Alice Houston

COPYWRITER

  • Hello, I'm Alice!

    I'm a Copywriter.

    Let’s not stop there though - You see, I’m obsessed with creation.

    My career consists of being a Professional Chef. My Speciality? Plant-Based Cuisine. The result of honing a variety of skills has led me to develop innovative dishes and an original menu for a Vegan Cafe in Birmingham City Centre, UK.

    ​Over the last year, my primal like craving for creativity has taken me back to my roots - writing, nurtured during my education studying Film Practice, where I quickly became an avid scriptwriter, producer and even sound designer.

    Now, I'm looking to work with clients who want to reach above and beyond success. Who aspire to grow and reach new heights together. As a Chef, I believe food possesses a universal ability to evoke emotion, summon memories and bring people together.

    Like Jonathan Safran Foer said “Food is not rational. Food is culture, habit, craving and identity.”

    Words are no different. So I’d like to extend my invitation to eat with you. Let’s make the world better together - one word at a time.
